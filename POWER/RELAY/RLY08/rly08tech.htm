RLY08 I2C/Serial Relay Module 

Overview
The RLY08 provides eight volt free contact relay outputs with a current rating of up to 1Amp each. It requires a 5v supply and has an exceptionally low maximum current consumption of approx. 100mA with all relays on. The relays are SPCO (Single Pole Change Over) types. The normally open, normally closed and common pins are all available on the screw terminals.

Operating Modes
There are two operating modes for the RLY08, I2C mode and Serial Mode. This is set with the Mode pin, and is connected to 0v Ground for Serial Mode or unconnected for I2C Mode. These are documented on individual pages. For I2C Mode click here, and for Serial Mode click here.
